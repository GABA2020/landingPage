About GABA

The first verified community for medical students and graduates of medical school. We offer reviews on educational resources, schools, and personalized career development.

First Year Medical Student

“The hardest part of being in medical school is getting adjusted to the workload, being that I was freshly out of undergrad, without any formal study plan/strategy.” – Rebecca, MS1*

Congratulations on your acceptance! Get started on the right foot by joining our community of veteran students who have already offered reviews of educational resources.

Second Year Medical Student

“There are so many resources that [it] can be overwhelming to go through, so having something that narrows it down is helpful.” – James, MS2*

You made it through first year! Let’s get you prepared to excel this year and perform well on USMLE Step 1 with our of educational resources and development portal.

Third Year Medical Student

“I wanted to a way to keep those anecdotes or stories, for example patient encounters that were particularly impactful and that might be helpful in my personal statement” – Benjamin. MS3*

Can you see the finish line? We can! Finish strong through STEP 2 with our reviews of educational resources, get started building your ERAS early with your personalized GABA profile, and plan your rotations with program reviews.

Fourth Year Medical Student
